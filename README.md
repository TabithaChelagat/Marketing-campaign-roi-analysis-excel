This project provides a deep-dive analysis into multi-channel marketing performance to optimize spend and maximize Return on Investment (ROI). ## üìä Business Questions Answered
1. **Channel Efficiency**: Which channels (Referral, Email, Social, Paid Ads) generate the highest revenue vs. spend?
2. **ROI Optimization**: Which specific campaigns should be scaled, and which are currently draining the budget?
3. **Regional Performance**: What are the ROI trends across different global regions?
4. **Budget Reallocation**: How can we reallocate spend from underperforming assets to high-efficiency winners?

## üí° Key Findings & Insights
* **ROI Powerhouses**: **Referral** (Avg. 31.0x ROI) and **Email** (Avg. 11.4x ROI) are the most efficient channels.
* **Standout Campaign**: The **"Affiliate Network"** campaign achieved a massive **61.3% ROI**, representing a clear opportunity for scaling.
* **Budget Leakage**: Identified that **"Google Ads - Local"** and **"Instagram Shopping"** are currently draining budget with negative or near-zero returns.
* **Regional Success**: Europe represents the most successful regional model, suggesting that its messaging and offers should be piloted in other markets.
